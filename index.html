Diplomat Hotel

Diplomat Hotel is a historic building situated atop Dominican Hill in Baguio City. It once served as a rest house and a seminary way back 1911 before it was developed into a school and eventually a hotel. Despite the ruins and being abandoned, the hotel remained one of the most picturesque spots in the City of Pines
